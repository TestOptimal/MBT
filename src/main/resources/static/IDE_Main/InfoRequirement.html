Define requirements to be covered by the model.

Use of Requirements:

  • Requirements can be referenced in TRIGGER script to track their coverage and test status. Ctrl-R or Cmd-R in Script Editor to open requirement selection list.
  • Requirement traceability can be visualized on model with Requirement View in MODEL tab.
  • Requirement coverage are reported in RESULT tab.

Additional Info: Requirements are saved in model's data folder. You may extend this capability to integrate with your own requirement management system.

More info at Wiki / Requirement Editor.
