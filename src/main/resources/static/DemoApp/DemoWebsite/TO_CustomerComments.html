References and Comments from TestOptimal Customers

These comments are from our customers/users who have used TestOptimal with great successes in their organization.

To whom it may concern,

I am pleased to recommend TestOptimal as a powerful tool in the emerging area of model based testing. Over the past 14 months TestOptimal has proven to provide excellent tools and efficient and friendly support.

Maintainability: When requirements change, TestOptimal is a far quicker way to update 100's of test cases. No need to re-record scripts.

Dynamically create manual test cases, output them as spreadsheets or web pages. A quicker and better way of generating manual test cases.

TestOptimal takes Behaviour Driven Developement (BDD) Testing to another dimension. BDD's Given - When - Then statements are easily converted to TestOptimals finite state machines. For Given - When - Then statements, TestOptimal is a better way of having different permutations, chaining statements together and using different data combinations. TestOptimal builds on benefit of BDDs to create a more powerful way of testing.

TestOptimal allows you to adjust the amount of testing certain actions undergo in your tests (weights). With guard statements, you manage the differening consequences of the same action in a different context.

People talk about navigating through a web site or a program. TestOptimal makes this easier to see through its inbuilt graphical model representations. Plus you can see a graphical animation of the path you take through a system when developing tests

James, Test Engineer

The navigation is 'dynamic' (you can change the sequencer engine easily) compared to other 'static' scripting tools. And exercising a SUT dynamically provides better coverage then repeating the same scripts every time.
